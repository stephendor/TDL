# RSS Editorial Query Draft

This is a draft only. Authorship is now fixed; send after the companion-paper
scope and cover-letter language are final.

## Suggested Subject

Companion submissions to JRSS-A and JRSS-B on persistent homology for
longitudinal panel data

## Suggested Body

Dear Editorial Office,

I am preparing two companion submissions for the Journal of the Royal
Statistical Society:

- a JRSS-A paper on UK career inequality, persistent homology, and Mapper-based
  mobility boundaries; and
- a JRSS-B paper on hypothesis testing and survey-design diagnostics for
  persistent homology on longitudinal social data.

The papers are designed to be self-contained and separately reviewable, but they
share a common empirical setting and will cite one another as companion pieces.
The plan is to submit them to JRSS-A and JRSS-B on the same day and to post the
corresponding arXiv preprints simultaneously.

Could you advise whether JRSS has any preferred cover-letter language for such
companion submissions, and whether coordinated handling across Series A and
Series B is appropriate or available?

Best wishes,

Stephen Dorman
The Open University, UK
