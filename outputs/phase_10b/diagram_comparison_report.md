## Interpretation: Why τ is Positive Pre-GFC but Negative Pre-COVID

### The Topological Mechanism

1. **GFC (τ = +0.88):** The financial system was experiencing a gradual
   **buildup of interconnected stress**. As subprime mortgages deteriorated,
   correlations between assets increased erratically, creating new topological
   loops (H1 features) that appeared and disappeared. This **increased variance**
   in topological complexity signals growing structural instability.

2. **COVID (τ = -0.80):** The pandemic was an **exogenous shock** that hit
   all markets simultaneously. There was no gradual buildup—instead, all
   assets became highly correlated very quickly. The topology became **more**
   **stable** (lower variance) as the market structure collapsed into a
   single correlated mass.
