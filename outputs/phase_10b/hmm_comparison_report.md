### Why τ May Not Improve HMM

1. **τ is a trend signal, not a level:** HMM detects regime levels (high/low VIX),
   while τ measures the *trend* in topological variance. These are different signals.

2. **τ is already partially captured by VIX:** The 21% correlation between τ and VIX
   means some information overlaps.

3. **HMM may not be the right model for τ:** A changepoint model or
   early warning system might better utilize the trend information in τ.
