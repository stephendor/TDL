### 1B. Rewrite §2.3 — H₁ Framing (Phase 3, Concern #10)

**Current framing:** H₁ loops as poverty-trap signatures.

**New framing:** Abandon the H₁–poverty-trap link. Key points:
- Poverty traps as absorbing states would manifest as dense H₀ clusters (high-density regions in the point cloud), not H₁ loops
- H₁ loops correspond to cyclical oscillation patterns in embedding space — individuals cycling between states
- The H₁ negative result (Markov-1 non-rejection for H₁) bounds the complexity of cyclical dynamics: "the trajectory manifold lacks persistent cyclical structure beyond what first-order Markov dynamics produce"
- This is a meaningful negative result, not a failure to find poverty traps

### 1C. Rewrite §4.3 Interpretation (Phase 3, Concerns #2, #3)

**Current framing:** Order-shuffle rejection "validates" GMM regime discovery.

**New framing:**
- **Demote order-shuffle:** The order-shuffle rejection confirms that temporal ordering creates geometric structure in the point cloud (connectivity, merge-tree shape). This is a necessary but unsurprising finding for autocorrelated time series — it does NOT validate the specific GMM density modes. Reframe as: "temporal ordering shapes the global geometry of the point cloud."
- **Foreground Markov-1:** The Markov-1 non-rejection is the primary informative finding. It establishes that the topological structure is fully accounted for by first-order transition probabilities. Frame as: "the total persistence of the trajectory point cloud — both connected components (H₀) and loops (H₁) — is statistically indistinguishable from that generated by a first-order Markov process."
- **Explicitly decouple from GMM:** State clearly that H₀ PH and GMM regime discovery operate on different geometric properties (connectivity vs density) and that the ARI between H₀ components and GMM assignments is near zero. They are parallel, complementary analyses, not one validating the other.

### 1D. Rewrite §5.1 — What TDA Adds (Phase 3, Concern #2)

**Current framing:** TDA produces "better" regimes than OM.

**New framing:** TDA's contribution is:
1. The geometric characterisation of the trajectory point cloud (Betti numbers, persistence)
2. The formal null-testing infrastructure (Markov memory ladder) that bounds the memory-complexity of the topology
3. A complementary perspective to sequence-analytic methods — not a replacement
